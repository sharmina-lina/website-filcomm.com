PRODUCTS


Founded in 1996, FILCOMM is one of the premier yarn companies specializing in sourcing fancy yarn.
FILCOMM prides itself in its extensive development and R & D services. We offer our clients the newest and most innovated fancy yarn and styling, along with general market analysis.
Major categories: Slub, Feather, Tape, Space Dyed, Brush, Multi color Nep, Tube, TT, Diversed twisted, Natural material yarn, Etc..

FILCOMM can do the entire sourcing process with trends analysis, from preproduction for sampling, manufacturing, final inspection, shipping, export documentation and customs clearance.
Based on long business experience in the fancy yarn field, We founded Sweater factory in Bangladesh. As a sweater manufacturer, we can offer good and stable quality and competitive price by using our own fancy yarns and having Jacquard machines of all gauges allows us to develop magnificent designs to satisfy our customers.
