Welcome to Filcomm

A leading player in the sweater industry with over 26 years of experience.

Our journey began in 1996 with a strong focus on delivering high-quality products and excellent customer service. Over the years, we have evolved and expanded our operations to include Filcomm China, founded in 2001, and S.S. Sweater Ltd. in Bangladesh, established in 2007. At Filcomm, we recognize the advantages of a vertical production model that leverages the strengths of both China and Bangladesh.

In China, we specialize in the production and export of fancy yarn to the Asian market. Our extensive range of yarns encompasses various materials and dyeing methods, including brushed, slub, tape, feather, tube, roving, boucle, T/T, and many more. In Bangladesh, S.S. Sweater Ltd. operates as a 100% export-oriented sweater industry. Our state-of-the-art manufacturing unit enables us to produce all kinds of sweaters, including pullovers, cardigans, knitted skirts, and scarves.

Thank you for choosing Filcomm. Our dedicated team of professionals works tirelessly to ensure that our products meet the highest quality and remarkable standards. We look forward to serving you with our exceptional products and unwavering commitment to your satisfaction.

ABOUT US

SERVICES

Founded in 1996, FILCOMM is the one of the most premier yarn company specializing in the sourcing fancy yarn and sweaters. FILCOMM prides itself in its extensive development and R & D services. Therefore, FILCOMM can do the entire sourcing process with trends analysis, from preproduction for sampling, manufacturing, final inspection, shipping, export documentation and customs clearance.

We have full time specialists who continuously research the market for the next trends. We offer our clients the newest and most innovated fancy yarn and styling, along with general market analysis.

Major categories: Slub, Feather, Tape, Space Dyed, Brush, Multi color Nep, Tube, TT, Diversed twisted, Natural material yarn, Etc..

Based on long business experience in the fancy yarn field, We founded Sweater factory in Bangladesh. As a sweater manufacturer, we can offer good and stable quality and competitive price by using our own fancy yarns and having Jacquard machines of all gauges allows us to develop magnificent designs to satisfy our customers.
